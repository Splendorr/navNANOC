Navigar Consulting improves business processes.

Through our proven methodology and the use of new and existing technologies, Navigar improves business processes resulting in cost savings, improved controls, and transparency.
 

Navigar is a derivative of the Latin word for Navigation, which is to plan, control, and record the position and course of a vehicle on a journey.

Much like a crew navigates a ship through the waters, Navigar will lead your organization through process improvements.
Navigar provides the expertise to improve organizational process performance for clients who are planning for, implementing a process improvement project, or moving to an environment of continual process improvement.
